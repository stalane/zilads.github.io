Community Driven Paid Advertising

Zilliqa Ad Network (ZilAds) intends to solve a particular problem than many community members believe is inherent in distributed blockchain ecosystems; that of how to appropiately and effectively engage in marketing and advertising. We will offer a solution to this with community-based paid advertising and marketing campaigns.

Supported Networks

Social Networks are the lifeblood of Crypto projects. We intend to buy advertising on behalf of the Zilliqa community to promote via target keywords in the following networks.

  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Google
  • Reddit
  • LinkedIn

Initially, we will build ads for the Zilliqa Blockchain and $ZIL token but will soon also build campaigns for other ZRC-2 projects in the ecosystem such as: $XCAD, $GZIL, $CARB, $PORT, $SCO, $BLOX etc.
